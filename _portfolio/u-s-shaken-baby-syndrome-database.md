<h2>U.S. Shaken-Baby Syndrome Database</h2>

<p style="color:black">Below is a database containing more than 3,000 U.S. shaken-baby syndrome cases, gathered and verified by The Medill Justice Project. The information can be filtered to find specific data. For example, you can isolate gender of the accused and location of the case. To view a geographic visualization of the data, click here. Information is updated periodically. The database was updated May 20, 2015.</p>
