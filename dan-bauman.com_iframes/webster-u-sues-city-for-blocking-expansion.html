"Tipping Point": The Road to the Lawsuit

By Dan Bauman, The Journal at Webster University

Webster University and Eden Theological Seminary sued the City of Webster Groves. The university and seminary allege the city unlawfully denied the university’s request to use property it had purchased from Eden. Webster had hoped to use two buildings on Eden’s property for university activities and demolish a third.
